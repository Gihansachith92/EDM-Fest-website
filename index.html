Biggest EDM Festivals in the World (2023)

One of the most popular music genres on the planet is Electronic Dance Music

Location: Boom, Belgium. Tomorrowland is perhaps the largest and best-known techno festival in the world. The festival even consists of a camping festival and the actual festival. The camping festival takes place the night before the actual festival. In addition to its huge lineup, Tomorrowland is also known for the fireworks show at the end. Definitely something you don’t want to miss, put it on your bucket list!

Location: Miami, Florida, USA. Ultra Music Festival (UMF) is one of the largest Electronic music festivals in the United States. This three-day festival attracts electronic music lovers from all over America every year. In the meantime has Ultra spawned a larger series of international franchises under the blanket branding ”Ultra Worldwide”. Ultra now has festival venues worldwide, such as in Croatia, South Africa, Singapore, Mexico, Brazil, and many other places.

Location: Las Vegas, Nevada, USA. Electric Daisy Carnival Las Vegas, also known as EDC Las Vegas, is an unmissable event for any electronic music fan! This annual three-day festival features an extraordinary lineup of world-renowned DJs and emerging (techno) talent. Every year, thousands of visitors from all over the world visit EDC Las Vegas. The stages are truly breathtaking, with mesmerizing designs and special effects that will leave you in awe.

Location: Cluj-Napoca, Romania. Untold Festival is one of the largest dance music festivals in Europe and attracts visitors from all over the world (120+ countries). Untold Festival is also called the EDM paradise of Eastern Europe. The four-day festival is held every year in the Cluj Arena in Cluj-Napoca, Romania. There are no less than 6 stages where the biggest names in techno will perform.

Location: Haarlemmermeer, Netherlands. As a Dutch native, I know how popular techno music is here in the Netherlands. There are a lot of different techno festivals throughout the year in this country, including Mysterland. Mysteryland is known for its enchanting green atmosphere surrounded by forest, with multiple stages set in the stunning Floriade Park in the Netherlands.

Artists Reviews

5 Best EDM Artists Of All Time

1. Tiësto

Tiësto (born Tijs Michiel Verwest, January 17, 1969) is a Dutch DJ and record producer of electronic dance music. Some of his achievements include the 2000 remix of Delerium’s “Silence” featuring Sarah McLachlan that made him gain mainstream popularity among EDM fans.

Review by EDM Rekords
2. Avicii

Avicii (born Tim Bergling, 8 September 1989 – 20 April 2018), was a Swedish DJ and record producer. At the age of 16, he began posting his remixes on electronic music forums, which led to his first record deal. He rose to prominence in 2011 with his single “Levels“.

Review by EDM Rekords
3. Skrillex

Skrillex (born Sonny John Moore; January 15, 1988) is an American DJ and music producer. He found club and mainstream stardom beginning in 2008, when he swapped his gig as the frontman in post-hardcore band From First to Last for the dancefloor-oriented project Skrillex.

Review by EDM Rekords
4. Swedish House Mafia

Swedish House Mafia is a Swedish house music supergroup consisting of Axwell, Steve Angello and Sebastian Ingrosso. They rose to prominence in the 2000s with their high-energy brand of EDM and progressive house music.

Review by EDM Rekords
5. Dimitri Vegas & Like Mike

Dimitri Vegas (Dimitri Thivaios) and Like Mike (Michael Thivaios) were born on 16 May 1982 and 3 December 1985 in Belgium. They left Belgium in 1999 to play a European tour and settled in Mallorca to establish their names on the European dance circuit before moving to Halkidiki, Greece and then Ibiza in 2003.

Review by EDM Rekords
